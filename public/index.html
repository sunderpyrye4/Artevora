About Artevora

Founded in the heart of Chicago, Artevora is a premier home décor consultancy specializing in contemporary urban style. Our philosophy is rooted in the dynamic energy of Chicago's architectural landscape, where industrial heritage meets modern sophistication.

We transform residential and commercial spaces into curated environments that balance form and function. Our signature palette—graphite black, ice white, and steel accents—creates a timeless aesthetic that reflects Chicago's bold spirit while providing warmth and livability.

Our team of seasoned designers brings a meticulous eye for detail and deep understanding of urban living. We work closely with each client to create personalized spaces that not only look exceptional but also enhance daily life through intelligent design solutions.
